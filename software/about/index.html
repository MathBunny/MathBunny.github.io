About Me

I am Horatiu Lazu, currently attending William Lyon Mackenzie CI. People who know me often characterize me by my love for problem-solving and leadership. I love trying new things, building, and organizing events.

I savour challenge, both in the form of programming and math. I love the aspect of competition and the motivation it creates for learning. Figuring out difficult problems and improving inspired me to continue competing and have seen strong improvements in my technical abilities because of it.

Equally as much I enjoy taking leadership and bringing new ideas to the table. While attending William Lyon Mackenzie CI I delivered a multitude of innovative ideas that used my skillset to construct and impact the lives of students.

Feel free to follow me on social media, this includes GitHub, Facebook , LinkedIn along with YouTube. You can also look at my résumé.

This is me! In my spare time I enjoy riding long-distance bike rides. I compete in Strava segments, do century-rides and train on hills.
